Who We Are

BORN FROM A
LOVE OF THE HILLS

Limuru Cycling Club started as a small group of passionate cyclists who wanted to explore the incredible terrain on their doorstep. What began with a handful of friends and a few bikes has grown into the premier cycling community in Kenya's central highlands.

Our shop — the Ex UK Shop, right opposite Njengi Plaza and the Bata Shoe Company in Limuru Town — was born out of necessity. We wanted quality bikes at prices that Kenyan riders could afford. So we started importing directly from the UK, selecting bikes with care and making sure everything was ride-ready before selling.

Today we're a club, a shop, a repair workshop, and a community all rolled into one. Whether you're a first-time rider or a seasoned cyclist, this is your home.

THE EX UK SHOP

Located in the heart of Limuru Town, directly opposite Njengi Plaza and the famous Bata Shoe Company — you can't miss us. Our shop is stocked floor to ceiling with quality pre-owned bikes imported directly from the UK.

Why ex-UK? British cyclists take care of their bikes, invest in quality equipment, and replace them regularly. That means we get well-maintained machines from trusted brands — Trek, Specialized, Giant, Cube, Carrera — at a fraction of new prices.

Outdoor Adventures · Limuru Highlands

TRAILS &
OUTDOORS

Kenya's Best Kept Cycling Secret

The Terrain

LIMURU AT
2,400M ABOVE
SEA LEVEL

Perched on the edge of the Great Rift Valley, Limuru offers cycling terrain that is genuinely world-class. The cool climate, rich red earth, tea estate paths, eucalyptus forest tracks, and sweeping views make every ride an experience.

Routes range from gentle scenic loops through tea gardens suitable for beginners, to demanding technical descents and multi-hour endurance rides for experienced riders.

All our guided rides include a local guide who knows the terrain, weather, and best stops — often including chai breaks at local tea estates.

TRAILS

⬤ 22km · Easy-Moderate · 2–3 hrs

Tea Estates Loop

Wind through endless green rows of tea in the Tigoni and Limuru tea belt. Scenic, peaceful and flat enough for all levels. A must-do on any first visit.

Kereita Forest Descent

A legendary descent into Kereita Forest with technical sections, river crossings, and dense indigenous forest. One of Kenya's best MTB trails.

🏞️Rift Valley Rim Photo
⬤ 50km · Advanced · 5–6 hrs

Rift Valley Rim Ride

Follow the dramatic escarpment edge with breathtaking valley views. Climbs, descents and some of the most spectacular cycling scenery in East Africa.

🌅Sunrise Ride Photo
⬤ 18km · Easy · 1.5–2 hrs

Sunrise Tea Garden Spin

Early morning gentle ride as the mist rolls over the tea estates. Perfect for beginners, families and anyone who wants to start the day right.

🛤️Kinale Ridge Photo
⬤ 40km · Intermediate · 3.5 hrs

Kinale Ridge Circuit

Rolling highland terrain through farming villages, past Kinale Forest and back around via the scenic Uplands road. A classic club route.

🏘️Village Trail Photo
⬤ 28km · Easy-Moderate · 2.5 hrs

Uplands Village Loop

A warm community route through highland villages, small farms, and local markets. Interact with residents and experience the real Limuru countryside.

Our Workshop

EXPERT
MECHANICS
ON SITE

Our fully equipped workshop is staffed by experienced bicycle mechanics who have seen and fixed everything. From a simple puncture to a complete frame rebuild — we handle it all, quickly and honestly.

Walk in, or book ahead for complex work. Most basic repairs are done same day. We use quality parts and will always show you what we've done and why.

We work on all bike types — mountain bikes, road bikes, hybrids, BMX, kids bikes, and fixies. Any brand, any age.

What We Fix

ALL REPAIRS
COVERED

01

Puncture Repair

On the spot tube patching or full tube replacement. In and out in 20 minutes.

02

Brake Service

Brake pad replacement, cable adjustment, hydraulic bleed, caliper alignment. Stopping power restored.

03

Gear Tuning

Indexing, cable replacement, derailleur adjustment. Smooth shifting across all your gears.

04

Chain & Drivetrain

Chain replacement, cassette swap, chainring work, bottom bracket servicing and replacement.

05

Wheel Truing

Wobbly wheel? We'll true it back to running straight. Spoke replacement and tension work too.

06

Full Service

Complete head-to-toe overhaul. Clean, inspect, service every component. Like a new bike when it's done.

07

Suspension Service

Fork inspection, seal replacement, oil refresh, travel adjustment. Keep your suspension working as designed.

08

Headset & Bearings

Creaky, loose, or worn headset? We'll clean, adjust or replace to get that solid steering feel back.

09

Bike Fitting

Saddle height, reach, handlebar position — proper fit prevents injuries and makes every ride better.

10

Custom Builds

Building a dream bike from scratch? Bring us your parts or we'll source them. We love a custom build.

11

Kids Bike Safety Check

Before a child rides, we check everything — brakes, tyres, chain, bolts. Free with any part purchase.

12

Rust Treatment

Highland humidity is brutal on bikes. We clean, treat and protect against corrosion to extend your bike's life.
